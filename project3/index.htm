How to Play League of Legends

On Summoners Rift, the map that League is played on, there are 3 lanes Top lane, Mid Lane, and Bot lane. The center of the map is divided by a river, and everything in between contains the jungle. Every lane has an outer tower, followed by an inner tower, followed by an Inhib tower, then an inhibitor, and finally 2 nexus towers guarding the Nexus. You and your team mates have to destroy enemy towers to gain access to their base, and ultimatly their nexus. At the start of the game you and your team spawn in your fountain at the back of your base, here you can buy items with gold you acquire as well as heal and restore your mana. You start the game at level 1 and 500 gold to buy a starting item. As you play the game you get experience to level up, and gold to buy more items in order to become stronger. You gather gold and experience buy killing minions, slaying enemy champions, destroying towers, or killing monsters in the jungle. Minions automaticaly spawn for each team at their nexus and march down every lane to help seige towers. If your team destroys an enemy inhibitor than super minions will spawn into that lane helping you sige into their base..

The Five Roles

  • Top Lane

    • Top Lane is the most isolated lane with a big focus on 1 verse 1 against their lane opponent. The traditional top lane champions include tanks, fighters, and duelists.

  • Jungle

    • The Jungle is perhaps the most unique role, because they are the only role whose primary gold and experience comes from the camps in the jungle, rather than the minions in lane. The traditional jungle champions include tanks, assassins, and fighter.

  • Mid Lane

    • Mid lane is one of the primary carry positions. Mid laners usually play high damage champions that can win teamfights or duels,but they a high risk of dying because they are less mobile and have less health. The traditionally mid lane champions include mages, or assassins.

  • ADC

    • ADC stands for Attack Damage Carry. ADC's are the bot laners who are the other primary carry like mid laners. ADC's are traditonaly the least unique in champion type because they are all ranged marksmen who like mid laners have very high damage, but are very easy to kill.

  • Support

    • Supports are also in the botlane with the ADC making botlane the only lane with two players. Their job is what you think, to support. They help their ADC get kills to make them strong, and help them avoid death from the enemies. However supports help everyone on their team, and are just as important as an other role. The traditional support champions include casters, mages, and tanks.

The Lore of League of Legends

The Universe of League of Legends mostly takes place in Runeterra. Runeterra has multiple regions from icy tundra, rolling plains, tall mountains, thick jungles, and hot deserts. Every champion in League of Legends has their origin. They come from all walks of life and come in many shapes and sizes. Many champions come from one the main factions below, but many come from places not charted on any map, like the void. In League there is a champion for everyone, you can play as a pirate, a sorceror, a gunslinger, a swordsman, a giant monster, a space dragon, a giant talking rat, or a cat, and many more. Each champion has their own personality which is expressed through their look, their playstyle, and their voicelines.

Major Factions and Regions

  • Freljord

    • Freljord is located in the north dominated by massive mountains, harsh winters, and lots of snow. People there form tribes in order to survive the harsh climate.

  • Demacia

    • Demacia is a small yet powerful kingdom located around the western planes. They are seen as a benevolent kingdom, but Demacians don't like magic, and are enemys of Noxus.

  • Noxus

    • Noxus is a large kingdom located on the eastern coast. Their leader, Swain, is a great conquerer in search of power, they are enemies of Demacia, and others.

  • Ionia

    • Ionia is a rather peacefull island whos inhabitants tend to be much more in touch with nature and magic. The vastaya come from here, they are humanoids who can have animal like features such as bird wings, or fox tails.

  • Piltover & Zaun

    • These two city stand next to each other seperated by large bay. Piltover is the most technologicaly advanced city in Runeterra, because they can make hextech tools and weapons, by infusing mundane items with magic. Zaun on the other hand is known for its criminal underground. Crime is rampant and the city is full of low life crooks.

  • Bilgewater

    • Bilgewater is full of large port citys located on an archepeligo. Here almost everyone is a sailor of some kind, wether they are a humble fisherman, a trader, or a pirate.

  • Shurima

    • Shurima is believed to be the oldest civilization in Runeterra. Their leader Azir rules over the great Shurima desert.

  • Targon

    • Targon is the largest mountain in Runeterra. It is very remote, and few people call it home. At the top of the mountain their are strange connections to celestial beings.

League of Legends Esports

League of Legends is the biggest Esport in the world. For ten consecuative years League has been hosting tournaments around the world for fans to watch. In League of Legends there are two major international tournaments a year, MSI, or Mid Season Invitational, and Worlds. MSI takes place half way through the year every summer, hense why it's called mid season, and Worlds takes place and the end of the year in October. The four main regions North America, Europe, Korea, and China all send their best teams to compete based on the winners of each regions seasonal tournament, in addition other smaller regions, like Brazil, Turkey, Oceania, or Japan each have a shot of fighting for the few remaining spots in the tournament. These events are huge worldwide. Hundreds of Millions watch from home, and stadiums like Staples Center, and Beijing Nation Stadium get sold out. The prize pool at Worlds is millions of dollars.
