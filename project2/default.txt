	<h2>What is League of Legends?</h2>
	<p>League of Legends is a free online multiplayer game for PC made by Riot Games. League of Legends, otherwise known as League,
 or LoL, is a MOBA or Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. In League you can play as 1 of 148 champions on a team with
4 other players against another team of 5. Both teams play on Summoners Rift where they battle each other in an
average 30 minute game with the goal of destroying the opponents Nexus. If you employ 
good strategy, strong mechanics, and a good attitude you will triumph over your competition.
</p>
