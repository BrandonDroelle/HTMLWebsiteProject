								<h2 class="major">Gallery</h2>
								<p>Welcome to The Gallery! Here you can view all of the previous submissions for each of our three categories, nature, wildlife, and urban. This gallery is constantly being updated with each new submission so be sure to come back often. If you wish to see which of the gallery images have won previous months contests then please go to the winners page. If you would like to vote for an image you see here than please go to the vote page.</p>
								<p>Each category showcased here is unique in it's own way. The nature category shows us nature in a broad view. It's meant to show off wide landscapes in order to appreciate the beuaty of all of nature as a whole, not just one small peice, but the entire ecosystem. The wildlife category is ment to show the amazing, smart, beutiful, creatures living in the wild. Having pictures of wild animals is great because it gives people a chance to see something they never would get the chance to, or an animal that is too dangerous to approach. The last category is urban. This category is important because it shows beauty in its own unique way. It contrasts with nature and shows of the marvels of human creation.</p>

								<h2 class="major">Rules</h2>
								<span class="image main"><img src="images/photography.jpg" alt="" /></span>
								<p>Brandon Droelle Photo Club hosts a monthly photo contest, where the user with the most votes from other users by the end of the month wins. We currently have three categories to enter, and each member can have one submission per category per month. You can vote for each category once, but you cannot vote for your own submission.
								<p><b>In order for your submission to valid it must apply to the photo clubs submission guidelines listed below</b></p>
								<p>
								<ul>
								<li>Your submission must be uploaded by the final day of the month by 11:59pm.</li>
								<li>Your submission must be one of the following file formats: png, jpg, or gif. All other formats will not be accepted.</li>
								<li>Your submission must be not be considered innappropriate.</li>
								<li>Your submission must fit in the the category it's being submitted for.</li>
								<li>Your submission must be your own original work, pladgerism will not be tolerated.</li>
								</ul>
